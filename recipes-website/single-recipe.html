Banana Pancakes

Wake up to the aroma of banana pancakes, a delightful twist on a morning classic that promises to turn your breakfast into a feast. Let the sweet scent of bananas and the warm, fluffy texture of pancakes start your day with a smile.

prep time

30 min

cook time

15 min

serving

6 servings

Tags: banana breakfast pancakes sweet

instructions

step 1

Begin by mashing ripe bananas in a bowl, creating a smooth base for your batter. The natural sweetness of bananas will infuse each pancake with a rich, irresistible flavor.

step 2

Gently fold in the flour, eggs, and milk, whisking to a velvety consistency. Watch as the batter comes together, promising golden pancakes that are both tender and tasty.

step 3

Heat a non-stick pan and pour in a ladleful of batter. As bubbles form and the edges crisp, flip your pancake to achieve that perfect golden-brown hue on both sides.

ingredients

2 ripe bananas

1 cup all-purpose flour

2 large eggs

tools

1 mixing bowl

1 whisk

1 non-stick pan
